APJ Abdul Kalam

Birth

APJ Abdul Kalam was born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, India.

A famous quote by the Kalam: Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action.

Education

APJ Abdul Kalam graduated in physics from the St. Joseph's College, Tiruchirappalli, and later he studied aerospace engineering at Madras Institute of Technology.
